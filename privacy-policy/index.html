Privacy Policy for Coin Grab

Last updated: July 28, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your privacy is protected when using our iOS game Coin Grab ("the Game").

Information Collection and Use

Coin Grab is a simple game where you click a coin on the screen. We do not collect any personal information from users of the Game.

Leaderboards

The Game includes leaderboards to enhance user experience. Any information displayed on the leaderboards is managed through Apple's Game Center service. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle this information.

Advertisements

The Game displays advertisements. These are provided by third-party ad services which may use certain device information and game usage data to serve relevant ads. This information is not used to identify you personally. For more information about ad targeting, please refer to the privacy policies of our ad service providers.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us so that we can delete that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
